Peanutbutter Celery

Back Home

Description

Healthy mid-day snack that can be made in a matter of seconds!

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Peanutbutter
  • raisins or other toppers

Steps

  1. Take your celery and wash under cold water.
  2. Cut the stem and butt end off of each piece of celery where the white starts.
  3. Using a knife, slather desired amount of peanutbutter onto the celery's length.
  4. If you want, you can add raisins or any other toppers
