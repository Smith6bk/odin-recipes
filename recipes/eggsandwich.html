Egg Sandwich

Back Home

Description

Perfect meal to start the day with that pairs well with a cup of coffee.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of choice
  • 2 eggs
  • Mayonaise
  • 1 tbls. cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat skillet and cooking oil on medium-high heat.
  2. Crack eggs and cook until opaque.
  3. Slather mayonaise onto the outside of each piece of bread.
  4. Lay bread mayonaise side down onto the skillet to toast with the cooked eggs now on them.
  5. If desired, add more mayonaise inside the finished sandwich
